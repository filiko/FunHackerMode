    Life Sciences 

    Sept. 19-21, 2025

    St. Paul, MN

     

    O4U Life Sciences (O4ULS) brings together exceptional undergraduate students with industry leaders at the forefront of research and development, pharmaceuticals, health technology, and other life science sectors. This event provides unique opportunities for students in biology, chemistry, bioengineering, neuroscience, genetics, health sciences, and more to connect with top sponsors in these cutting-edge fields. Join us for a weekend of professional development, networking, and recruitment, as we empower the next generation of innovators in life sciences.
